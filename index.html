Our Story

In the summer of 1999, we opened a small pet shop in the heart of our town. We were both animal lovers and had a dream of creating a place where pet owners could find everything they needed to take care of their furry friends. At first, business was slow, but word of mouth spread and before they knew it, their little shop was thriving.

As their reputation grew, customers came from all over the state to buy pets and supplies from them. Now, over 20 years later, Jane and David's pet shop is a beloved fixture in the community and they continue to provide top-quality products and services to pet owners near and far.
